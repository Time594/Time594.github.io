About Us

Founded in 2016, Worlducation is a social startup that manufactures tablet computers for primary school students. Our focus is not only on the hardware, but also creating software, content and activities to better engage and educate primary school students.

Worlducation aims to change the way children learn at school by implementing artificial intelligence technology that can follow up on each child’s progress and adjust to their needs as they learn, creating the optimal path learning experience. Worlducation envisions a world in which every child learns how to read and write – a world without illiteracy. This is why, for each classroom that buys our product, we donate and train a classroom somewhere around the world that can’t afford the same technology. We then connect the classrooms together so that they can grow and collaborate throughout their learning cycle.

Our Products

Our main business is to produce tablets specifically designed for primary school education. These tablets not only have rich educational resources and applications, but also incorporate specialized designs to ensure safety and suitability, assisting primary school students in their learning and growth.

We also provide customized educational solutions to meet the personalized needs of different schools and institutions.

Solutions

There are several solutions that Worlducation offers to address the challenges in primary education:

1.Innovative tablet computers: Worlducation manufactures tablet computers specifically designed for primary school students. The tablets are equipped with educational resources and applications that are tailored to the needs of young learners. The tablets also incorporate specialized designs to ensure safety and suitability for children.

2.AI-assisted learning: Worlducation aims to change the way children learn at school by implementing artificial intelligence technology that can follow up on each child's progress and adjust to their needs as they learn. This creates a personalized learning experience for each student, enabling them to achieve their full potential.

3.Content creation: Worlducation creates engaging and interactive content that is designed to better engage primary school students. The content is aligned with educational principles and is tailored to the needs of different age groups.

4.Classroom donations: For each classroom that buys Worlducation's product, the company donates and trains a classroom somewhere around the world that can't afford the same technology. This ensures that more children have access to quality education and helps to promote fair distribution of educational resources.

5.Collaborative learning: Worlducation connects classrooms together so that they can grow and collaborate throughout their learning cycle. This promotes cross-cultural learning and encourages students to develop a global perspective.

6.These solutions are aimed at addressing the challenges in primary education and creating a brighter future for children worldwide. By combining technology, content creation, and social responsibility, Worlducation is making significant strides towards achieving its vision of a world without illiteracy.

Worlducation: Revolutionizing Primary Education through Technology

Introduction:

Founded in 2016, Worlducation is a social startup that aims to transform primary education by providing tablet computers and innovative educational solutions to primary school students. By leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) technology, Worlducation aims to personalize the learning experience, promote global collaboration, and eradicate illiteracy.

Challenge:

The traditional education system often fails to engage and meet the individual learning needs of primary school students. Limited resources, outdated teaching methods, and lack of access to technology pose significant challenges to quality education worldwide. Additionally, the digital divide exacerbates educational inequalities.

Solution:

Worlducation has developed a holistic solution that combines hardware, software, content, and activities to enhance the learning experience. The company manufactures affordable and durable tablet computers specifically designed for primary school students. These tablets come pre-loaded with interactive educational apps, multimedia content, and engaging activities.
Key Features and Benefits:

AI-Powered Personalized Learning: Worlducation's tablets are equipped with AI algorithms that adapt to each student's progress, identifying their strengths and weaknesses. This personalized approach ensures optimal learning outcomes.
Global Collaboration: Worlducation connects classrooms worldwide, facilitating communication, collaboration, and cultural exchange among students. This fosters a sense of global citizenship and broadens students' perspectives.
Donations and Training: For every classroom that purchases Worlducation tablets, the company donates devices and provides training to underprivileged classrooms. This philanthropic approach aims to bridge the educational gap and create equal opportunities for all children.
Implementation and Impact:

To date, Worlducation has deployed its solution in numerous schools across different countries. The company has partnered with local education authorities, NGOs, and international organizations to identify high-impact locations for donations and implementation.
The impact of Worlducation's solution has been significant. Improved student engagement, enhanced academic performance, and increased literacy rates have been observed in classrooms adopting the technology. The AI algorithms have proven effective in adapting to individual learning needs and promoting personalized growth.
Conclusion:

Worlducation's innovative approach to primary education has the potential to revolutionize learning worldwide. By providing affordable technology, personalized learning experiences, and fostering global collaboration, Worlducation empowers students and educators to overcome educational barriers. Through their philanthropic efforts, they are making strides towards achieving their vision of a world without illiteracy.
